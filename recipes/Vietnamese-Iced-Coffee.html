Vietnamese Iced Coffee Recipe

A strong Vietnamese iced coffee makes an unbeatable Southeast Asian treat. Even those who only take their coffee black will like this. Serve it on hot summer days or as an evening treat. We learned this in Vietnam, and now drink almost all our espresso this way.

It's recipe is made with coffee mixed with sweetened condensed milk and chilled on ice. It's a relatively quick and easy to make recipe only taking 5 minutes to prep and 5 minutes to make.

Ingrediants for one serving:

  • 4 cups water
  • ½ cup dark roast ground coffee beans
  • ½ cup sweetened condensed milk, divided
  • 16 ice cubes

Steps:

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Brew water with coffee using your preferred method to make coffee.
  3. Spoon 2 tablespoons sweetened condensed milk into each of 4 coffee cups.
  4. Pour 1 cup fresh hot coffee into each cup and stir to dissolve the milk.
  5. Serve cups of coffee along with 4 tall glasses filled with 4 ice cubes each and a long-handled spoon.
  6. Pour hot coffee over ice cubes and stir briskly with the long-handled spoon to chill the coffee.
